Return and Exchange Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Thank you for shopping with Asaba Jumah. We strive to provide high-quality products and excellent customer service.

Return Eligibility

Customers may return both defective and non-defective physical products within 30 days of delivery.

To be eligible for a return:

  • The item must be in its original condition.
  • The item must be unused and undamaged.
  • The item must include all original packaging and accessories.
  • Proof of purchase or order confirmation must be provided.

Defective or Damaged Products

If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery.

Include:

  • Your order number
  • A description of the issue
  • Photos showing the defect or damage

After review, we will provide instructions for return, replacement, or refund.

Exchanges

We accept exchanges within 30 days of delivery.

Products may be exchanged for:

  • A replacement of the same item
  • A different item of equal value (subject to availability)

Exchange requests must meet the eligibility requirements listed above.

Return Process

To initiate a return or exchange:

  1. Contact us at the email address below.
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return.
  3. Wait for return authorization and instructions.
  4. Ship the item to the address provided by our support team.

Unauthorized returns may not be accepted.

Refunds

Once the returned item is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved:

  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method.
  • Processing times may vary depending on your payment provider.
  • Shipping fees are generally non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Return Shipping Costs

  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs for non-defective products.
  • We will cover return shipping costs for defective, damaged, or incorrect items.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return:

  • Downloadable digital products and ebooks after successful delivery or download
  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Products marked as final sale

Contact Information

For returns, exchanges, or questions regarding this policy, please contact:

Asaba Jumah
Website: https://www.asabajumah.top

We are committed to resolving customer concerns promptly and fairly.
