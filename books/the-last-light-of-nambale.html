About The Book

In a forgotten village surrounded by darkness, one young dreamer refuses to give up hope. “The Last Light of Nambale” follows the inspiring journey of Kato, a determined boy who leaves his home in search of a way to restore electricity to his struggling community. Filled with courage, innovation, and heart, this powerful story explores leadership, resilience, and the belief that even one person can change the future. Written by Ugandan actor and artist Asaba Jumah, this inspirational story encourages readers to dream bigger and become a light in their own communiti es.

Set within the legendary Nambale, the book follows hidden truths, unexpected encounters, and the power of survival against fear.

Written by Asaba Jumah, the novel explores imagination, suspense, and storytelling inspired by African creativity and cinematic atmosphere.
