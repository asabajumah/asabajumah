About The Book

I am Asaba Jumah is a personal autobiography that tells the life, creative journey, and ambitions of Ugandan artist Asaba Jumah.

The book explores his experiences in music, acting, storytelling, digital publishing, and entertainment while revealing the challenges and determination behind his success.

Written with honesty and inspiration, the book motivates young readers, artists, and dreamers to believe in their talents and pursue their goals despite obstacles.
