About The Book

The Smiling Man in Room 13 is a suspenseful horror story filled with mystery, fear, and supernatural secrets hidden inside an old boarding school.

As Ethan uncovers the terrifying truth behind Room 13, he must survive strange shadows, midnight footsteps, and a horrifying figure that refuses to disappear.

Written by Asaba Jumah, the novel blends psychological horror, mystery, suspense, and dark storytelling into an unforgettable experience for young horror readers.
