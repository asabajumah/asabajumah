About The Book

The House Beneath Black Hill is a chilling supernatural horror story filled with mystery, darkness, suspense, and terrifying discoveries.

When a group of teenagers enters the abandoned mansion hidden inside Black Hill Forest, they uncover ancient secrets connected to disappearances, evil rituals, and a terrifying creature trapped beneath the house.

Written by Asaba Jumah, the novel combines psychological horror, paranormal fear, and suspenseful storytelling inspired by cinematic horror.
