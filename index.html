Asaba Jumah

Biography

Asaba Jumah is a Ugandan actor, musician, and author of multiple books including: The Last Night of Nambale, The Smiling Man in Room 13, The Whispers of Kalenga Forest, I am Asaba Jumah, and The House Beneath Black Hill.
